Telecommunication AND Engineering

The Department of Telecommunication Engineering focuses on producing graduates with strong fundamentals in Telecommunication domain, which was established in the year 2000. The department consists of well qualified and dedicated Teaching and supporting staff. The department focuses on building the fundamentals in the field of Communication like wired and wireless. It has a total intake of 60 students and Telecommunication Engineering graduates have achieved a placement of 90% on an average during last 3 years. The salary packages are up to a maximum of around 5 Lakhs per annum. Companies like Tech Mahindra, IBM, CGI, HP, Amazon, J-Spiders and many more, offer placement opportunities. Students are motivated to participate in research projects in the areas of their interest.

The Department has undertaken many research projects funded by KSCST. The department has a total of 11 teaching faculty members with different specializations, out of which 1 is PhD holder and 6 are pursuing PhD, remaining are post graduates.

Faculty

Microcontroller Lab

Well equipped lab with higher end computer systems
Sufficient number of hardware kits to realize Real time applications like LED s, LCD s are available
Keil, MSP430 workbench are installed in all systems
Power back up facility for all systems

DSP Lab/HDL Lab

This lab implements the signal processing concepts Using MATLAB software
This lab also gives an introduction to DSP architecture
this lab is provided with high end systems capable of DSP related tasks
real time experiments are also implemented using DSP processor TMS320C6713 through code composer studio

Microprocessor Lab

Well established labs with high and systems
Adequate number of hardware components like ADC, STEPPER MOTOR, LOGIC CONTROLLER, etc
Skilled technical faculty
Systems with PCI slats for conducting interfacing programs

CCN Lab

Well equipped lab with projector faculty
Skilled technical faculty
Facilities realize protocols likes TELNET, RLOGIN, MAILBOX, FTP etc
Good ambience and well maintained

Analog Electronics and circuits Lab

Sufficient number of equipments like CRO, signal
Good ambience generators, power supply, dread boars etc
Skilled technical faculty

MICROWAVE AND ANTENNA LAB

Microwave lab paves way as a platform for students to explore microwave characteristics and various microwave devices (GUNN, KLYSTRON, wave guides)
In this lab microwave measurement technique for power, frequency, wavelength. Standing ware ratio we measured
Antenna parameter measurements end carried out for horn antennas & dish antennas

Digital Electronics Lab

Adequate number of trainer kits available
Sufficient number of IC s available
Facilitates the realization of theoretical concepts like, Flip flops, counters, logic gates, MUX, registers
PSPICE software is installed in all the computer systems

Advanced Communication Lab

To get better understanding of digital modulation techniques, micro strip and concepts of OFC losses
To understanding different advanced modulator techniques like QPSK, DPSK etc
Understanding & obtaining the radiation pattern of microwave antennas
Understanding the importance of various linear integrated circuits
